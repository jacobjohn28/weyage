Quick overview
Itinerary
Add cities and plan each day with spots — sights, restaurants, cafés, and experiences. Drag to reorder. Park unscheduled ideas in the Wishlist.
Overview
A magazine-style spread of your whole trip. Tap any city card to jump to it in the Itinerary. City AI briefings live here too.
AI Recommendations
Tap ✨ on any day in the Itinerary to get smart suggestions for restaurants, sights, and experiences — tailored to your city and style.
Budget & Costs
Attach a cost to any spot when adding it — or log standalone expenses in the Budget tab. See totals by category in your trip currency.
Documents
Attach booking screenshots and tickets to any spot. Use images (JPEG, PNG, WebP) — screenshot PDFs before uploading to keep files small.
Share & Collaborate
Generate a read-only link anyone can open without logging in. Or add collaborators in Trip Settings to let them plan alongside you.
Detailed guide
Overview tab
  • A visual, magazine-style spread of your whole trip — dates, cities, and key stats at a glance.
  • Tap any city card to jump directly to that city's days in the Itinerary.
  • Each city card has an AI Briefing toggle — generates a quick summary of what to expect (requires Gemini API).
  • City cover photos can be set or changed using the camera icon on each card.
Itinerary tab
  • Add a city using + Add a city at the top or the button at the end of the itinerary.
  • Each city is broken into days. Tap a day to expand it and add spots.
  • A spot can be a sight, restaurant, café, experience, or transport leg.
  • Tap any spot to open its detail drawer — view address, notes, cost, and attached documents.
  • Drag spots up and down to reorder them within a day.
  • Wishlist: spots not yet assigned to a date live at the bottom of each city. Drag them onto a day when you're ready.
  • Navigate between cities using the sidebar (only on desktop) or city cards in the Overview tab.
AI Recommendations ✨
  • Tap the ✨ sparkle button at the right edge of any day header in the Itinerary.
  • The AI panel slides in with tailored suggestions — restaurants, sights, hidden gems — based on the city and date.
  • Tap any suggestion to instantly add it as a spot on that day.
  • Use the text prompt at the bottom to refine: e.g. "budget-friendly lunch near the old town".
[Ignore for now]Requires a Gemini API key — set by the admin in Site Settings (from the All Trips page). Free keys are available at Google AI Studio.
Transit
  • Between cities: Add a spot with type Transport for point-to-point journeys to log a flight, train, ferry, or car journey. Enter carrier, seat, departure and arrival times.
  • Within a city: each day has transit chips (🚇 metro, 🚌 bus, 🚕 taxi…) — log how you moved around that day. These are editable in the budget tab.
Budget & Costs
  • When adding or editing a spot, fill in the Price field to attach a cost directly — it shows up in the Budget tab automatically.
  • Expenses are categorised: Food, Transport, Accommodation, Activities, Shopping, Other.
  • All amounts are shown in your trip currency. Set the currency in Trip Settings.
  • The Budget tab shows a total spend and a breakdown by category.
Guides tab
  • Auto-generated quick-reference cards for every spot in your trip.
  • Search by name, or scroll through all spots organised by city.
  • Each card shows category, address, notes, cost, and tags at a glance.
  • Great for offline reference on the day — no need to dig through the itinerary.
Documents
  • Open any spot's detail drawer and tap Attach to upload a screenshot or photo — booking confirmations, QR codes, tickets, etc.
  • All attachments for a trip are also visible together in the Documents tab, organised by spot.
  • Tap any thumbnail to open it full-screen.
Images only (JPEG, PNG, WebP) — avoid uploading PDFs as they are large and slow to load on mobile. Instead, take a screenshot of your PDF and upload that image.
Sharing & Collaboration
  • Share Itinerary (sidebar/menu) generates a read-only link — anyone can view without logging in. Great for sharing with friends or family.
  • Shared viewers can see the Itinerary, Overview, Budget, and Guides but cannot make changes.
  • To let someone edit the trip, add them as a collaborator: open Trip Settings (⚙ gear icon) → Collaborators → enter their email.
  • All edits by collaborators sync in real-time.
